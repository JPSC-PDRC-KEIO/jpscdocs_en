2.1. Which changes occurred in your household in the past year (October 2017 to September 2018)?¶

Pleas select all that apply.

  1. There was a birth.
  2. My parent(s) or my husband’s parent(s) moved in with us.
  3. A member of my household came home from a long-term posting elsewhere for work.
  4. My household increased in size for other reasons.
  1. A member of my household moved out because he or she was transferred for work.
  2. A member of my household moved out.
  3. A member of my household died.
  4. My household size became smaller for other reasons.
  1. I got married and formed a different household.
  2. I got divorced or separated and formed a different household.
  3. I (or my household) moved out of my (or my husband’s) parents’ house to form a different household.
